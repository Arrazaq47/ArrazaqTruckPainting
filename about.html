Who We Are

Professional truck, tanker & industrial coating solutions built for durability and performance.

Since 2018 our team has worked under the banner of Liquip NSW in Smithfield. On the 1st July 2025 the business re-branded to FuelSpec Services.

Being seen purely as a general service provider was limiting our ability to fully support the transport industry. To better reflect our expertise and commitment to quality, we established Arrazaq Truck Painting Services as a dedicated truck painting and refinishing specialist. Located in Nigeria, abebi street agbo malu apapa, Lagos State, and close to major transport and industrial hubs, we are best known for delivering high-quality truck painting, fleet refinishing, body restoration, and protective coating solutions that keep vehicles looking professional and road-ready

FuelSpec Services is owned and managed by a dedicated team of experienced professionals with decades of hands-on expertise in truck painting, refinishing, and heavy vehicle restoration. In 2018, the business was established with a clear vision — to provide high-quality, durable, and professional paint solutions tailored specifically for trucks and fleet vehicles.

Built on a strong foundation of technical skill and industry experience, FuelSpec Services combines craftsmanship with modern refinishing technology. Our leadership team brings together over 25 years of combined experience in heavy vehicle surface preparation, spray painting, colour matching, protective coatings, and fleet branding solutions.

FuelSpec Services reflects our shared passion for quality workmanship, innovation, and attention to detail. Our goal is to improve the appearance, longevity, and professional image of transport fleets and individual truck owners through premium painting and refinishing services.

Supporting transport operators, logistics companies, contractors, and independent truck owners, FuelSpec Services focuses on precision spray painting, accident resprays, full truck restorations, rust treatment, and protective coating applications. We understand that your vehicle represents your brand on the road, and we are committed to delivering finishes that stand out for the right reasons.

Our mission is simple but ambitious — to deliver durable, high-quality, and visually striking paint solutions that enhance fleet image, protect vehicle surfaces, and ensure long-term performance under demanding road conditions. By combining advanced spray technology with expert craftsmanship, we ensure every project meets the highest industry standards.

FuelSpec Services recognises that in today’s fast-moving transport industry, quality and reliability matter more than ever. Our workshop is fully equipped to handle everything from minor touch-ups to complete truck resprays. Every project is approached with precision, care, and a commitment to excellence.

Our large workshop service centre brings together a highly skilled team of spray painters, panel beaters, fabricators, and heavy vehicle specialists.
