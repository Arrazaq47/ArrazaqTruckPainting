Products and Services

Our Range of Products & Services

At Arrazaq Truck Painting, we specialize in providing high-quality truck painting, refinishing, branding, and restoration services. Our modern workshop and skilled team of painters are dedicated to delivering exceptional craftsmanship, attention to detail, and outstanding customer service.

We offer a complete range of truck painting solutions, including full vehicle resprays, custom paint designs, fleet branding, body repairs, touch-ups, and protective coatings. Whether you own a single truck or manage an entire fleet, we ensure every vehicle leaves our workshop with a professional and durable finish.
Using premium materials and advanced painting techniques, Arrazaq Truck Painting is committed to enhancing the appearance, value, and longevity of your vehicles while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.

A leading truck body styling and painting specialist.

Arrazaq Services specialises in professional truck and tanker painting solutions designed to meet the demanding standards of heavy-duty and industrial vehicles. We deliver high-quality surface preparation, spray painting, refinishing, touch-ups, and full respray services. As a full-service truck paint workshop, we cater to fleet operators,transport companies, and industrial clients, provision durable, high-gloss finishes that enhance appearance, protect against corrosion,and extend vehicle life.

We specialise in professional truck painting, repainting, and refinishing services for trucks and fleet vehicles, delivering durable finishes and quality workmanship to keep your vehicles looking professional on the road.
